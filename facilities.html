Boxing Equipment

The right equipment is essential for those looking to train like a boxer. A variety of gear is available to help improve skills and get in shape. At GymBox we have all the boxing equipment you can imagine including:

  • Boxing Gloves
  • Hand Wraps
  • Punching Bags
  • Jump Ropes
  • Focus Mitts
  • Headgear
  • Speedball
Photo: Boxing Gloves / Credit: Pexels
 

Boxing Ring

A full-size boxing ring is an essential component for any boxing gym, boxing event, or professional boxing match. GymBox Ring:

  • Ring Size: 20ft x 20ft
  • Ring Height: 4ft above ground
  • Ring Floor: Wooden
  • Padding: The ring has padded cushions around the edge of the ring floor.
  • Lighting: Well lit during the day time and night time hours.
  • Steps: Provided at each corner of the ring

Experienced Boxing Professionals

Experienced boxing trainers are the backbone of any successful boxing gym. At our gym, we pride ourselves on having the best trainers in the business, each with years of experience in coaching and competing at the highest levels of the sport.

Our trainers understand that each athlete has unique strengths and weaknesses, and they work tirelessly to create personalized training plans that help boxers of all skill levels reach their full potential. From honing your technique to improving your conditioning, our trainers will be with you every step of the way, providing the guidance and support you need to succeed.

So whether you're a seasoned pro or just getting started, our team of experienced boxing trainers is here to help you take your skills to the next level. Join our gym today and let us help you achieve your boxing goals!

As the legendary boxer Muhammad Ali once said:

"The fight is won or lost far away from witnesses - behind the lines, in the gym, and out there on the road, long before I dance under those lights."

Our experienced trainers know this all too well and will help you put in the hard work and dedication needed to achieve success in the ring.
