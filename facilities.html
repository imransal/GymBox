Boxing Ring

Photo: Boxing Ring / Credit: Pexels

A full-size boxing ring is an essential component for any boxing gym, boxing event, or professional boxing match. The following information will provide an overview of the key features and requirements of a full-size boxing ring. At GymBox our Boxing Ring has the following specficiation:

  • Ring Size: 20ft x 20ft
  • Ring Height: 4ft above ground
  • Ring Floor: Wooden
  • Padding: The ring has padded cushions around the edge of the ring floor.
  • Lighting: Well lit during the day time and night time hours.
  • Steps: Provided at each corner of the ring
