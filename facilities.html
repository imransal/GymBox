Boxing Equipment

At a boxing gym, the right equipment is essential for those looking to train like a boxer. A variety of gear is available to help improve skills and get in shape. The most common types of equipment include boxing gloves, hand wraps, punching bags, jump ropes, focus mitts, headgear, and mouthguards. Gloves come in different weights and styles, while wraps are worn under gloves to protect hands and wrists. Punching bags improve power and endurance, while speed bags improve hand-eye coordination and quickness. Focus mitts improve accuracy, timing, and combination punching, and headgear and mouthguards protect against impact. In addition to these basic items, other training aids such as medicine balls, resistance bands, and plyometric boxes may also be available. Whether a seasoned pro or just starting out, having the right gear can make all the difference in training at a boxing gym.
